What is Ray Tracing?
Ray tracing is a powerful rendering technique used in computer graphics to create incredibly realistic images. It simulates the way light travels in the real world, allowing for accurate lighting effects and shadows.

Course Structure
This course is designed to take you on a journey from the fundamentals of ray tracing to creating your own stunning images. We will build your knowledge step-by-step, ensuring a solid understanding of the core concepts. This beginner-friendly course offers 13 lectures on the basics and 8 coding practices to help you create impressive images.

Learning with Python
The course utilizes Python for all exercises. This choice makes the course accessible to a wider audience, even those with no prior programming experience. Python's clear syntax and extensive libraries make it a perfect language for learning ray tracing.

8 Weeks of Ray Tracing Practices
The course material is divided into manageable 8-week practices. Each week will introduce new concepts and provide hands-on exercises to solidify your understanding.

Course outline
This course offers a comprehensive ray tracing curriculum, starting with foundational concepts and progressing to intermediate and advanced topics.
